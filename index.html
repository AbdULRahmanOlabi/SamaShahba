Who We Are

Sama Al Shahba International Virtual School It is The First Licensed Virtual School By the Ministry of Education of the Republic Syrian Arabic in Aleppo Governorate. The School has a Distinguished Human Cadre With High Qualifications at The Scientific and Technical Levels.

In Addition, The School Seeks To Develop its Students’ Skills Through Many Methods Among The Activities Such as Mental Arithmetic and Virtual Laboratories, The School Teaches The Curriculum The Syrian One Followed in The Syrian Arab Republic and Approved By The Ministry of Education in The Republic Syrian Arabic, Under The Supervision of the Ministry of Education, Through Virtual Sessions and Classes Learning Via Distance.
